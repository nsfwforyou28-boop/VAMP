Biography

Born into nobility in the twilight of the medieval age, Lady Seraphina Nocturne was transformed on the eve of her coronation, becoming the first vampire queen to rule openly over both mortal and immortal subjects. Her reign has spanned nearly seven centuries, marked by an unprecedented era of peace between the living and the undead.

Her court in Castle Nightshade has become a sanctuary for those seeking refuge from persecution and a center of dark arts scholarship.

Immortality is not a gift to be squandered on petty vengeance, but a responsibility to guide the ages toward wisdom.

Legacy

Queen Seraphina's influence extends far beyond her territory. She has mentored countless vampires in the responsible use of their powers and established the Great Library of Eternal Night, which houses forbidden knowledge spanning millennia. Her diplomatic efforts have prevented numerous wars between supernatural factions, earning her respect even among her enemies.
